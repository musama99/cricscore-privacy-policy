Privacy Policy

CricScore: Cricket Scoring · Version 1.0.0 · Last Updated: July 2026

Effective Date: July 2026

CricScore is an offline-first cricket scoring app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

1. Introduction

Thank you for using CricScore: Cricket Scoring. This Privacy Policy explains how CricScore handles information when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

CricScore does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Payment information

3. Match Data

When you score cricket matches, the app may store match-related information locally on your device, including:

  • Match names
  • Team names
  • Player names
  • Scores and wickets
  • Batting and bowling statistics
  • Match history
  • Generated scorecards, posters, and reports

This data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

4. PDF Reports and Match Posters

CricScore allows you to generate PDF match reports and match posters. These files are created on your device. If you choose to share them, they are shared only through the apps or services you select.

CricScore does not upload your reports or posters to any external server.

5. App Permissions

CricScore may request storage or media-related permissions depending on your Android version. These permissions are used only to save or share scorecards, match posters, and PDF reports.

6. Internet Usage

CricScore is designed to work offline. An internet connection is not required for scoring matches, viewing saved match history, generating scorecards, or creating reports.

7. Data Sharing

CricScore does not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share user data with advertisers
  • Upload match data to external servers
  • Share personal information with third parties

8. Data Security

Match data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining access to your device and creating backups if needed.

9. Children’s Privacy

CricScore does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not require accounts or collect personal information, no personal data from children is knowingly stored or shared.

10. Data Deletion

You can delete saved matches inside the app where supported. You can also remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the app from your device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
